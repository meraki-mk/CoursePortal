Change of Course
Verzeo allows you to change your course to an alternative course only once using the following guidelines:

1. Verzeo is notified in writing at least 1 week before the commencement of the original course
2. The alternative course has seats available and will be finished within 1 month of the original course finish date
3. If switching to course of a higher value, students are required to pay the amount difference. However, if switching to a 
course of a lower value, Verzeo is not liable to pay the amount difference. A processing fee of Rs. 500/- needs to be paid within
 3 days after Verzeo accepts change of course request, failing which course change request will be cancelled and no further request
  will be entertained
4. Course change is allowed only once during the program

Process for Change of Course
1. Contact Verzeo at support@verzeo.in with your course change request
2. Once approved, fill up the “Course Change Form” and submit it within 3 business days
3.Pay the processing fees + difference in course costs (if applicable) and submit the receipt to “ support@verzeo.in ”
4. In 5-7 business days, you will get a confirmation for the course change request

 or can choose to join a different batch/course
